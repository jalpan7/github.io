Terms & Conditions

1. Volunteer Role and Duties

As a volunteer, you will be expected to contribute to various activities, including but not limited to event coordination, administrative tasks, and assisting with community outreach. It is important to follow the guidelines set by the organization and complete the tasks assigned to the best of your ability.

2. Compensation and Expenses

Volunteering with our organization is a non-paid position. Volunteers will not receive monetary compensation. However, reasonable expenses such as travel or meal allowances may be reimbursed, subject to approval by the organization before the event or task is undertaken.

3. Training and Orientation

all Volunteers will be provided with training and orientation to help them understand their roles and responsibilities. This training may include online resources, manuals, or in-person sessions organized by the volunteer coordinator.

4. Support and Communication

Volunteers will have access to ongoing support from a designated team member. Volunteers are encouraged to communicate regularly, ask questions, and request assistance when necessary to ensure a positive and productive experience.

5. Insurance and Liability

During the course of your volunteer activities, you will be covered by the organization's insurance policy. This coverage includes liability protection and injury coverage while volunteering. Specific insurance details can be provided upon request.

6. Volunteer Conduct and Boundaries

Volunteers are expected to maintain a high level of professionalism and respect in all interactions. Personal boundaries should be respected, and the organization's values and code of conduct should always be upheld. Volunteers should approach any concerns or issues respectfully and professionally with the appropriate personnel.

7. Modifications to Terms

The organization reserves the right to change these terms and conditions at any time. Any changes will be communicated to volunteers in advance, and continued participation will be considered acceptance of the updated terms.

By participating as a volunteer, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to these terms and conditions. We are excited to have you join us in making a positive impact in our community!
